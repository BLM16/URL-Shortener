URL Shortener takes a URL, gives it a "key" and then redirects https://my_domain.com/key to the original URL. This is useful because it prevents massive URLs from cluttering up documents, emails, or whatever else you are making. Instead, you can just link a nice, short and clean URL that allows you to get your point across while looking proffesional at the same time. Say goodbye to URL cluttered documents and emails!
URL Shortener is useful for buisnesses where they can have short links for all of their services, rather than massive ones that slow down workflow.
